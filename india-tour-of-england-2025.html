India Tour of England 2025 — Test Series

Published : August 18, 2025

The Anderson–Tendulkar Trophy has delivered four enthralling Tests. England currently lead the series 2–1, setting up a high‑stakes finale at The Oval. Below are crisp summaries from each venue and what the decider could hinge on.

1st Test – Headingley, Leeds (June 20–24)

England pulled off a demanding chase of 371, winning by five wickets. India’s hundreds from Shubman Gill and Rishabh Pant set up the game, but England’s lower order, aided by a burst from Josh Tongue, wrested the momentum late to go 1–0 up.

2nd Test – Edgbaston, Birmingham (July 2–6)

India struck back emphatically with a 336‑run victory—among their most dominant wins in England. Captain Shubman Gill’s majestic 269 underpinned the batting, while Akash Deep’s six‑for and Mohammad Siraj’s support finished the job.

3rd Test – Lord’s, London (July 10–14)

A classic Lord’s finish tilted England’s way by 22 runs. Ravindra Jadeja’s fighting 61* kept India alive, but young spinner Shoaib Bashir produced the decisive breakthrough to restore England’s lead at 2–1.

4th Test – Old Trafford, Manchester (July 23–27)

England piled up 544/7 to seize control, stretching the advantage to 186. Joe Root’s 150 elevated him to second on the all‑time Test run charts. Ben Stokes’s all‑round imprint was telling, while India lost rhythm after Pant retired hurt.

Series Status & Final Match

  • Series: England lead 2–1 with one to play.
  • India positives: Gill’s form, middle‑order resistance, and Jadeja’s finishing acts.
  • England edge: Batting depth, home conditions, and high‑tempo “Bazball” intent.
  • India watchpoints: Pant’s fitness, workload on seamers, and consistency with the new ball.

What to Expect at The Oval (July 31–Aug 4)

The Oval often rewards spin as the game progresses. India may lean on all‑round options and dry up runs through control, while England’s in‑form top order and proactive tactics aim to clinch the trophy. Expect tactical adjustments, selection calls, and a result pitch.
