Kendra Scott Announces Engagement

Published : August 18, 2025

Jewelry mogul Kendra Scott is engaged to longtime partner Dr. Thomas Evans after a lakeside proposal in Lake Como, Italy. The couple shared sun‑drenched photos from the moment, prompting a flood of congratulations from fellow designers, philanthropists, and fans across social media.

The Ring

In a nod to her signature aesthetic, Scott’s ring features a luminous oval‑cut center stone set on a sleek yellow‑gold band with subtle pavé accents. While the exact specs remain private, the design reflects Kendra Scott’s modern‑classic sensibility—elegant, wearable, and deeply personal.

Relationship Timeline

  • 2021: Scott and Evans are first linked, keeping their relationship largely private.
  • 2022: The couple make select public appearances and support several Austin‑based causes.
  • 2023–2024: Joint philanthropic work grows; the pair are spotted at industry events and charity galas.
  • 2025: Romantic proposal in Italy; the couple confirms the engagement on social media.

Public & Industry Reactions

Support poured in from the fashion, design, and wellness communities. Fans highlighted Scott’s commitment to philanthropy and mentorship—hallmarks of her brand—as reasons the news resonated beyond celebrity circles.

What’s Next

Early chatter points to a spring 2026 celebration with family and close friends—potentially in Austin, where the brand is headquartered. Meanwhile, Scott continues to expand into home and lifestyle categories, with new collections slated for later this year.
